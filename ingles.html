Systems for Internet

The Superior Course of Technology in Systems for Internet has the purpose of offering to its students superior, gratuitous and quality education, providing to the technologists knowledge and integral formation, based on the tendencies of the contemporary competitiveness, making them able to intervene in the development Economic and social development of the region in which the course is located.

The main objective of the course is to train technologists capable of:

  • Elaborate projects of information systems for the Internet
  • Raise needs for the technical specification of projects and their development
  • Implement different types of computational applications for the Internet
  • Administer and maintain information systems, especially those geared towards the Internet
  • Evaluate and test software
  • Manage human and technological resources
  • Assist in the graphic design of interfaces for computer applications for the Internet
  • Building Information Systems for the Internet
